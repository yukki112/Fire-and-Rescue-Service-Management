About Us

Protecting Quezon City 2025

QCprotektado Fire & Rescue Service Management has been at the forefront of emergency response and fire prevention in Quezon City for over three decades. Our dedicated team of firefighters, paramedics, and rescue specialists work tirelessly to protect lives and property.

We operate 28 fire stations strategically located throughout Quezon City, ensuring rapid response times to emergencies. Our state-of-the-art equipment and continuous training programs enable us to handle a wide range of emergencies, from structural fires to technical rescues.

Beyond emergency response, we are committed to fire prevention through public education, building inspections, and community outreach programs. Our goal is to create a fire-safe community where residents and businesses can thrive without fear of fire-related disasters in 2025.
